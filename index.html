The Five Pillars of Islam

Shahada

The Shahada is the first step to becoming a Muslim. Ash-hado= I bare witness. Ana la illaha illa Allah= That there is no god worthy of worship, but Allah. Wa Ana Muhammad Rasool-Allah= And that Muhammad is the prophet.

Sawm

Sawm is an arabic word for Fasting. Muslims fast on a special month called: Ramadan. They fast from sunset and break their fast at sundown.While fasting, there are many things Muslims should be aware of:

  1. They cant eat or drink.
  2. No listening to music.
  3. No backbitting or lying.
  4. You cant hear or watch bad things.
  5. God is always watching you.
  6. This is a month for doing good, and earning deeds.
